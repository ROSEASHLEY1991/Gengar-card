Gengar

Shadow Pokémon

Gengar is known for its mischievous and sinister nature Ghost/Poison-type weak against Dark, Ghost, Psychic, and Ground-type moves. Evolves from Haunter when traded. Haunter evolves from Gastly. Gengar possesses several abilities, including Levitate, which makes it immune to Ground-type moves.

Dark Secret

One of the most popular creatures in the series, Gengar, was also once a human. The final evolution to Gastly is mostly known for casting curses and haunting others – in addition to making environments colder. Nevertheless, there is a mention of its humanoid form in the Moon entry registered by Bulbapedia.
